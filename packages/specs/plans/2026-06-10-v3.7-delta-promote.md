## Design decisions (probed + ratified)

1. **The ledger lives in the kernel record-store** (USER-ratified) — alongside the candidate genesis
   records, content-addressed + INV-22 tamper-evident. A lab store would be a forgeable second copy of
   a kernel-attested fact; the record-store is the un-forgeable home.
2. **Anti-forgery by producer != decider** (RP-9). The mint records the INTEGRATOR's stack/quarantine
   decision, bound to the candidate's kernel-attested `post_state_hash` — never a caller-asserted
   verdict. An agent cannot mint its own absorb.
3. **Candidate-level only at v3.7** (RP-13). Requirement-tagging + coverage-reconciliation defer to
   post-intake (intake is unbuilt). The v3.7 ledger entry keys on the candidate, not a requirement.
4. **Consumers honor OQ-NS-6 (binding law).** At v3.8: reject-rate -> breaker (trust-DOWN, narrowing —
   demote a high-reject persona; gated on these EVIDENCE-LINKED kernel-attested rejects per hacker
   H-ATK-6). Absorb-rate -> reputation **NARROW-only + display** (force MORE review on a high-reject
   persona); it may **NEVER harden a skim** — only a world-anchored merge (v3.9 external signal) hardens.
5. **`recorded_at` is FS-mtime-backed + fail-closed on `excluded_future`** (C1 canon) — never a
   caller-chosen timestamp.
6. **NEVER-TOUCH-HEAD stays binding + v3.7 stays SHADOW.** The ledger MINT is a record-store append
   (writes no git ref); the ledger RECORDS, never gates. The VALIDATE grep is scoped to the W1 diff +
   asserts the mint writes no ref.

## Threat-model declaration (W2 deliverable)

Human-gated delta-promote is safe under the **cooperative, trusted-local-filesystem, single-uid,
human-in-loop** posture. The hostile-same-uid residual is NOT closed: a same-uid caller can `utimes()` a
reject-event record's mtime **INTO THE PAST** to age it out of the breaker window — and the
`excluded_future` check does NOT catch this (it only flags FUTURE-dating; the storm-hide direction is
the past, **UNMITIGATED at the FS layer** — VERIFY H1, live-probed; the v6 canon agrees: "back-date
evades"). Plus worktree != sandbox. These close only at the ContainerAdapter (Track 2 / OQ-E). Shadow
stays the default; the path is opt-in / human-invoked.

## Methodology notes

- **Mechanical extraction first**: frontmatter + H2 structure extraction via `awk` and `grep` covered 80% of the audit surface efficiently. Targeted Read calls applied to representative docs (single-responsibility, spawn-conventions, plan-mode-hets-injection, react-essentials, identity-roster, stack-skill-map) validated the mechanical findings and surfaced details (e.g., `## Phase` body content in architecture KB).
